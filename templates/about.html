About the Party

Background, Vision, and Mission

Nagkakaisang Tugon Party Alliance is a student-led coalition formed to represent engineering students through credible leadership, clear communication, and concrete service.

Background

Why the alliance was formed

The party was established in response to the need for more accountable, organized, and student-first leadership in the Engineering Student Council. Our members believe that real representation requires presence, planning, and a willingness to act on student concerns.

Vision

An empowered engineering student community

We envision an Engineering Student Council that promotes inclusive growth, practical innovation, and a campus culture built on competence, integrity, and service.

Mission

Responsive and transparent service

We commit to translating student concerns into real programs, strengthening academic and welfare support, and keeping the student body informed through clear communication.

Core Values

Accountability, unity, discipline, and action

These values guide how the alliance organizes, communicates, and leads. Every initiative should be measurable, student-centered, and grounded in collaboration.
